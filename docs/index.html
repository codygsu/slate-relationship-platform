MEET YOUR ACADEMIC HOME

Meet Your College — Review Hub

In the undergraduate experience, students who indicate a program of interest will see a “Meet Your College” page tailored to their academic home. This widget is one of several portal components, but we’re starting here because it sets the tone: you belong here, and you can picture yourself in the community that will support your goals.

How To Review

Use the links on this page to open each college page in a new window (so it mirrors how the content is experienced in-context). Review the content, video embed, and calls-to-action as a cohesive “welcome to your academic home” moment.

  • What we’re testing Clarity, warmth, credibility, and whether the page creates a strong sense of “academic home.”
  • What to look for Voice/tone, accuracy, usefulness of CTAs, and whether the experience feels supportive and easy to navigate.
  • How to send feedback Use the Email Feedback button on this page (it pre-fills the subject line).
