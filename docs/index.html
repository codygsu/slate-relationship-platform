Meet Your College — Review Hub

In the undergraduate experience, students who indicate a program of interest will see a “Meet Your College” page tailored to their academic home. This widget will be one of many portal components, but we’re starting here because it sets the tone: you have an academic home at Georgia State.

How To Review

Use the links on this page to open each college file in a new window. This mirrors how the “Meet Your College” content will be experienced inside the portal, while keeping this hub available as your navigation and instructions.

  • What we’re evaluating Does the page feel welcoming, clear, and credible — and does it create a sense of belonging and direction?
  • What to check on each page Voice/tone, clarity of value points, video embed behavior, CTA usefulness, and overall “academic home” feel.
  • Where to send feedback Use the Email Feedback button on this hub (pre-fills the subject line).
